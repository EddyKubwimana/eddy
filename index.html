About Me

I am a Computer Science graduate (Cum Laude) from Ashesi University. As CEO and Founder of PayVista, I lead a mission to empower mobile money agents and underserved communities in Burundi. We’ve expanded to 5 provinces with over 600+ agents, serving more than 200,000 customers.

My journey combines technical expertise and entrepreneurial drive. I’ve raised over $15,000 in grants, joined the ImpactVest Alliance, and grown our team from 2 to 12. I specialize in mobile app development, scalable fintech infrastructure, and bridging the gap between traditional and digital finance.
